Privacy Policy

Court Pop · Last updated 13 July 2026

Court Pop is a scheduling app for a private group of padel players. This page explains what information the app collects and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Phone number — used to verify your identity when you sign in, and to let other players invite you to a specific game.
  • Name — what you enter the first time you sign in, shown to other players in games you're part of.
  • Game details you create — date, time, location, and similar details for games you organize or join.

How this information is used

Solely to operate the app: to sign you in, show you the games you're part of, and let you invite or be invited to games by other users. It is not used for advertising, and it is not sold to anyone.

Who it's shared with

Your information is stored using Google Firebase, the backend infrastructure this app runs on. Firebase processes data on our behalf as our cloud provider, governed by Google's own security practices. We don't share your information with any other third party.

Visibility to other users

Your name and phone number are visible only to other users in a game you're both part of. Nobody can see any of your other games unless you've specifically invited them and they've accepted.

Data retention and deletion

Your information is kept for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact animesh202@yahoo.com .

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address.
